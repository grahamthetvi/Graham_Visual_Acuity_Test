About This Tool

This calculator determines equivalent Snellen visual acuity from an object's physical size and its distance from the viewer. It also categorizes the viewing distance (Near, Intermediate, Far) and reports the expected accommodative demand.

Clinical Logic & Formulas (For Professionals)

Visual Angle: Uses the exact geometric split-triangle formula to determine the subtended angle in arc-minutes:

2 × arctan(size / (2 × distance)) × (10800 / π)

Equivalent Acuity: Derives the Snellen denominator based on the standard 5 arc-minute letter:

(visual_angle_minutes / 5) × 20

Accommodative Demand: Calculated in Diopters based on viewing distance:

1 / distance_in_meters
